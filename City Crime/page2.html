The body in the apartment




Detective Jake pulled up to 3:45 in the Old Town,an upscale apartment complex.
A call had come in—Johnson, a 42-year-old investment banker,
found dead in his living room.
A single gunshot wound to the chest. No sign of forced entry.

Jake scanned the crime scene. The front door was locked.
A half-drunk cup of coffee sat on the table.
The TV was still on. Beside the victim’s hand—a torn movie ticket stub for ‘Last Night in New York’ (9:30 PM show).

"Someone was here with him," Jake muttered.
